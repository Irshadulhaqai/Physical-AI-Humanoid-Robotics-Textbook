# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics** course — a comprehensive 13-week journey from foundations to building an autonomous humanoid robot.

## Course Overview

| Weeks | Module | Topics |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 1-2 | Introduction | Physical AI foundations, humanoid landscape, hardware overview |
| 3-5 | Module 1: ROS 2 Jazzy | Nodes, topics, services, packages, URDF |
| 6-7 | Module 2: Digital Twin | Gazebo, physics simulation, sensors, Unity bridge |
| 8-10 | Module 3: NVIDIA Isaac | Isaac Sim, perception, navigation, reinforcement learning |
| 11-13 | Module 4: VLA & Capstone | Voice-to-action, cognitive planning, humanoid fundamentals |

## Who This Course Is For

- **Industry practitioners** with Python programming knowledge
- **Robotics enthusiasts** wanting hands-on experience with modern AI-powered robotics
- **Students** preparing for careers in Physical AI and humanoid robotics

## Hardware Requirements

Choose one of three supported configurations:

1. **Digital Twin Workstation** — Gaming PC with NVIDIA RTX GPU
2. **Physical AI Edge Kit** — NVIDIA Jetson for edge deployment
3. **Cloud-Native Setup** — Cloud-based development without local hardware

See the [Hardware Setup Guide](/docs/hardware-guide) for detailed instructions.
